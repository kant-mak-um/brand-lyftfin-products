Overview

LyftFin develops composite fins for surf and SUP, plus modular hydrofoil components for wing foiling. Products are designed to be serviceable and expandable as skills and conditions evolve.

Early sales may occur via eBay as market tests. Ecommerce and memberships will be handled by WaterWolf (private platform).

Modular hydrofoil concept

Beginners often need a wider, higher-lift wing. As skills, wave type, and wind speed change, smaller wings can unlock higher performance. LyftFin will support a modular pathway to evolve setups over time.
