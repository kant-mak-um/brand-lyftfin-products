# brand-lyftfin-products

**Brand:** LyftFin  
**Domain:** https://lyftfin.com (apex) and https://www.lyftfin.com  
**Category:** Composite fins and modular hydrofoil components (surf, SUP, wing foiling)

## Purpose
This repository hosts the public-facing product definitions, documentation, and marketing-ready assets for **LyftFin**.

LyftFin focuses on:
- **Surf + SUP fins** (performance and durability)
- **Wing-foiling components** (foils/wings and related modular parts)

## Modularity concept (early)
Beginner-friendly hydrofoils often benefit from **wider, higher-lift wings**, while advanced conditions and skills favor **smaller, faster wings**. LyftFin will support a modular pathway where foil components can evolve with the rider.

## Open Designs / Closed Process
Designs are intended to be open source. Manufacturing processes and production parameters are not published and remain proprietary.

## Commerce & Memberships
Early sales may occur via eBay as market tests.  
Ecommerce and memberships will be handled by **WaterWolf** (private platform).

- `/catalog/` – product placeholders (to be populated as SKUs and accessories are finalized)
- `/LICENSE` and `/LICENSES/` – licensing scaffold (per your standard)

## Status
Pre-launch. Content will expand as initial fin and foil SKUs become available.
